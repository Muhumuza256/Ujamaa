About Ujamaa

Ujamaa General Agencies (U) Ltd is a fast-growing, youth-led enterprise in Uganda. Over 90% youth-owned and operated, Ujamaa is driven by the spirit of creativity, togetherness, and growth.

We empower young professionals to deliver innovative and reliable solutions across industries - from sourcing essential goods to crafting high-performance sportswear and impactful brand visuals.

Our skilled team ensures seamless ground operations, customized product sourcing, and client engagement, making Ujamaa a trusted partner for organizations seeking youthful energy, professionalism, and creative excellence.
